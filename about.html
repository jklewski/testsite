Cicular Building Sector Conference 2025 (CBSC 2025)

The 1st International Circular Building Sector Conference, 1-3 June 2025

About the conference

The conference is organized by the Faculty of Engineering at Lund University, Profile Area Circular Building Sector. In line with the mission of the Profile Area, the conference aims to contribute to new knowledge on circular solutions and approaches, bridging the gap between industry and academia. The conference topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Circular materials and components
  • Circular design and buildings
  • Circular use and business models
  • Circularity in higher education

The program (tba) will include a welcome reception, keynote speeches, research sessions, a conference dinner, and a field trip. Stay tuned for a Call for Abstracts coming soon!

Please note that the program is preliminary and subject to change. The early-bird conference fee is planned to be 7 000 SEK, including welcome reception, conference sessions, site visit, dinner, lunch, and coffee. PhD students presenting a full paper will receive a discount.
