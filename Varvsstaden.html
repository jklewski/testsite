Circular Building Sector Conference 2025 (CBSC 2025)

The 1st International Circular Building Sector Conference, 1-3 June 2025

Varvsstaden

Join us for a guided visit to Varvsstaden – one of Sweden’s most ambitious urban transformation projects. Situated on the former Kockums shipyard, Varvsstaden is being developed into a vibrant, sustainable city district. The project stands out for its reuse of materials, integration of industrial heritage, and innovative approaches to circular construction. This visit offers a unique opportunity to explore how large-scale redevelopment can combine architectural quality, sustainability, and practical reuse in a contemporary urban context.

Chartered buses will depart from the conference venue in Lund. Don't forget to sign up for the tour during registration!

Circular Building Sector

A circular building sector will drastically reduce its energy use, carbon dioxide emissions, generation of waste and use of virgin materials.
